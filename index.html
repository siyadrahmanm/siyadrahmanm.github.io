Hello! I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the IITB-Monash Research Academy, working under the guidance of Prof. Neela Nataraj (IIT Bombay) and Prof. Ricardo Ruiz Baier (Monash University).

I am originally from Nilambur, Kerala. I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics at MES Mampad College (Autonomous), University of Calicut, followed by a Master’s degree from IISER Thiruvananthapuram.

My research interests lie in partial differential equations, numerical analysis, and finite element methods.
