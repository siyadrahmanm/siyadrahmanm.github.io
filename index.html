Hey! I am a Mathematics PhD student at the IITB-Monash Research Academy. My supervisors are Prof. Neela Nataraj (IIT Bombay) and A/Prof. Ricardo Ruiz Baier (Monash University).

I hail from Nilambur, Kerala. I completed my Bachelor's in Mathematics from Dr. Gafoor Memorial MES Mampad College, University of Calicut, and my Master's in Mathematics from IISER Thiruvananthapuram.

My research interests include partial differential equations, numerical analysis, and finite element methods.
